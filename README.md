# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard developed in Microsoft Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and workforce trends.

The dashboard helps HR managers identify key factors influencing employee attrition and supports data-driven decision-making for improving employee retention.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition.
- Monitor workforce demographics.
- Identify departments with high employee turnover.
- Analyze salary distribution.
- Understand the impact of overtime on attrition.
- Support HR decision-making.

## Business Insights

- Overall employee attrition rate is 16.1%.
- Research & Development has the highest employee attrition.
- Employees aged 26–35 experience the highest attrition.
- Employees working overtime are more likely to leave.
- Most attrition occurs in the lowest salary slab.
- Sales Executive has the highest attrition among job roles.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Reduce excessive overtime.
- Improve employee engagement.
- Review compensation strategies.
- Strengthen career development programs.
- Focus retention initiatives on high-risk departments.
